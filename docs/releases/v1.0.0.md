The first public release of **Gangland (Text Edition)** — a single-player, text-driven tribute to MediaMobsters' *Gangland* (2004), written entirely in C against the raw Win32 API. No C runtime, no frameworks, no asset files: one ~130 KB executable that depends only on Windows system DLLs.

## The game

You are Mario Mangano, sent to Paradise City in 1932 to avenge your brother Chico by hunting the three brothers who killed him — while building a criminal empire and a family to inherit it.

- **Campaign** — errand boy → capo soto → the three-brother vendetta, with Vincenzo's kidnapping as the mid-game act break (and, later, a chance to bring him home).
- **Rackets & rivals** — extort or seize 14 businesses across five districts; three named rival capos track grudges, flip your rackets, raid your safe house, and can be appeased at a sit-down or retired permanently.
- **Dynasty** — court a wife (wine and dancing lowers the dowry), wed her under armed escort, raise up to three heirs whose type and quality follow their mother's traits, tutor them, and — if you die — continue as the next generation.
- **The family underworld** — mistresses and suspicious wives, in-law tribute, kidnappings and ransoms, informants in your own crew.
- **The law** — the Chief's recurring shakedown, heat that scales with district police presence, and a bomber who can remove the law from a map entirely.
- **Presentation** — a dark art-deco Win32 GUI with a collapsible district map (event pings, war borders, neighborhoods), GDI-drawn family silhouettes and rank stars, and procedurally synthesized period sound.

## Download

Grab `gangland-v1.0.0-win-x64.zip` below, extract, and run `gangland.exe` (Windows 10/11, x64). The binary is unsigned, so SmartScreen may ask you to confirm — More info → Run anyway. Saves are written next to the executable.

## Building from source

Open `Gangland.sln` in any Visual Studio with the C++ workload, or run `build.bat`. Compiles clean at `/W4`, links with `/NODEFAULTLIB` — the C runtime is not used anywhere.
